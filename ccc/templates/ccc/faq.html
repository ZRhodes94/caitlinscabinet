Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How long do your custom products take?

    2. Timing depends on the specific item and the length of the waitlist. When I reach out to discus your commission form, I will provide you with a more accurate timeline.

  2. Can I request a completely custom product?

    3. I currently only add customizations to my current products.

  3. If I remove components from an item, does the price become reduced?

    4. I will not increase the price for removing a component, but the price will not be further reduced for this customization.

  4. What is your refund policy?

    5. Based on your provided timeline, I will provide you with the date that you can return your order for a complete refund. Past this date, you will pay a deposit for your product to ensure payment for my work. Once a deposit has been submitted, you will no longer be able to return your item for a complete refund.

  5. What happens if my item is lost in the mail?

    6. I track all of my items. If your item is lost in the mail, I will reach out to discuss your options. I can either remake your item- given that you are willing to wait for completion. Or I will provide a complete refund.
